Are these stock avatars from a library? +
No. Every avatar we build is custom-designed for your brand based on your patient persona — look, voice, ethnicity, age, vibe, wardrobe, and setting. Your avatars are exclusive to you and never resold or recycled.
How long does delivery take? +
Avatar Starter delivers in 10 business days. Avatar Growth delivers in 21 business days. Avatar Authority ships the upfront 60-video library in 21 business days, then ongoing 20 fresh videos/mo.
Can I use these avatars on TikTok, Meta, and YouTube? +
Yes — videos are delivered in vertical 9:16 format optimized for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts, plus square and horizontal cuts on request for Meta feed and YouTube.
Do I need FTC disclosure on AI avatar ads? +
AI avatars are not endorsements from real people, so traditional influencer FTC disclosure rules don't apply. We handle all standard ad disclosure requirements during creative review.
What if I don't have brand assets yet? +
No problem. Our intake form walks you through everything we need — patient persona, brand voice, treatment focus, and creative direction. We can build from minimal starting assets.
Can I upgrade from Starter or Growth to Authority later? +
Yes — your purchase credits roll over toward an Authority tier upgrade within 90 days. We'll keep your existing avatars and expand the library.
